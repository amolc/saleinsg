FAQ's

1. Why should I sell on TradeExchange.co?

TradeExchange.co is the e-commerce engine for 100% export to other countries We would help with online reach for your products to Singapore, India, Malaysia, Thailand, Bangladesh and Vietnam.

2. Who can sell on TradeExchange.co?

Anyone selling new and genuine products is welcome. In order to start selling, you need to have the following:

  • Personal Identity Card - Passport or any Government ID
  • Address Proof - Telephone Bill/Passport
  • Bank account
  • Minimum of 5 unique products to sell

3. How do I get enquiry on TradeExchange.co?

To sell on TradeExchange.co:

  • Register yourself at TradeExchange.co.
  • List your products under specific product categories.
  • Once an enquiry is received, Please check Messagebox and your email with details.

4. How do I sell on TradeExchange.co?

To sell on TradeExchange.co:

  • Register yourself at TradeExchange.co.
  • List your products under specific product categories.
  • Once an order is received, pack the product and send it to customer.
  • Once an order is successfully dispatched and a delivery proof is updated. Buyer can confirm delivery and release of payment.
  • TradeExchange.co will settle your payment within 7 business days based on your seller tier.

5. Can I be a buyer and seller on TradeExchange.co?

Yes, you can be a buyer and seller on our system.

6. Who decides the price of the products?

As a seller, you will set the price of your products.

7. Will I get charged for listing products on TradeExchange.co?

The number of listings are based on the plan which is selected. For eg.

  • 10 for free listings
  • 100 for premium
  • 500 for partner.

8. How and when will I get paid?

The payment will be made directly to your bank account or credit card. TradeExchange.co will settle your payments within 7 business days once client has accepted delivery.

10.When can I start selling?

After all the required documents have been verified and your seller profile is complete, you can start listing your products and start selling.

11. How many listings are required to start selling?

You are required to have a minimum of 1 listings(unique products) to start selling on TradeExchange.co
